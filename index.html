App Overview

Wealth Maxtor App does all the work for you.

Wealth Maxtor is offering a service that is unique and has never been done before in the financial services industry. All you need is 10 minutes to set up and follow these 5 easy steps:

01

Download the application from the Google Play Store.

02

Register with just an email address and choose a password.

03

Log in with the same credentials.

04

Press Overview to set your pay schedule (weekly/bi-weekly/monthly) and your next pay day.

05

Add your expenses and earnings.
