Features

Wealth Maxtor App does all the work for you.

Saving

Figure out how much you should be saving every paycheque! The app calculates all your expenses and your earnings to help you save to the fullest potential!

Simply add all of your expenses and I mean ALL, including your daily coffee, breakfast, eating out, partying - everything! Now, I understand that it may be hard to determine how much some of your expenses will cost. Some nights you may end of paying $50 partying and some night over $100; therefore, we suggest that it would be best if you were determine how much you'd like to spend and do not spend more than that amount!

Pay Reminder

We can all get carried away with our busy life to remember to pay our bills and to put away a portion of our earnings to save. Thankfully, we have added a reminder function in the app on your pay days!

Now you can make sure to save your money and pay your bills on time!

Investments

See how much your savings would grow through investments from banks or a financial institution.

If you are doing awesome and saving your money then why not invest?! If you're smart with your investments, you could have twice the amount of your savings and retire early!

Wishlist

Saving for something you really want has never been easier! The wish list feature allows you to calculate how much money you need to put away every paycheque to get your dream car, house, or shoes! Best part? You can add as many wish list items as you'd like!

Simply enter the item you'd like to have in a certain amount of months and the cost of that item! For example: I hope to own a car in 20 months and the car I'd like to get will cost me $2000.

As soon as you enter your wish list, the app will calculate how much you will need to save every paycheque in order for you to be able to get your item within the specified month!

The calculation will be saved under your expenses and will also be calculated within your "Should Be Saving" amount!

Good luck and I hope you get what you deserve!
